🏥 AI OPD Management & Insurance Claim Optimizer

📌 Project Overview

AI OPD Management & Insurance Claim Optimizer is a healthcare analytics and operations management system developed during my internship at Pheme Software Pvt. Ltd.

The project focuses on improving Outpatient Department (OPD) operations and insurance claim processing through data-driven analysis. It simulates a real-world healthcare environment by managing patients, doctors, appointments, billing records, and insurance claims while generating actionable business insights.

The primary goal of the project is to help healthcare organizations monitor operational performance, identify bottlenecks, reduce claim rejection rates, and improve overall patient and financial management.

---

🎯 Key Features

- Patient Registration & Management
- Doctor and Department Management
- Appointment Scheduling & Tracking
- Billing and Revenue Management
- Insurance Claim Processing
- ETL Pipeline & Data Validation
- KPI Reporting and Business Analytics
- Claim Rejection Analysis
- Operational Performance Monitoring

---

📊 Project Highlights

- Managed data for **500+ patients**
- Processed **2000+ appointments**
- Generated **1450+ billing records**
- Analyzed **1200+ insurance claims**
- Implemented ETL validation checks to ensure data accuracy and consistency
- Generated KPIs related to OPD performance, billing efficiency, and insurance claims
- Identified major claim rejection reasons and operational bottlenecks

---

📈 Business Insights

The system provides insights into:

- Appointment completion and no-show trends
- Average patient waiting time
- Revenue generation and payment status
- Insurance claim approval and rejection rates
- Claim processing turnaround time
- Insurer performance comparison
- Department-wise operational efficiency

These insights help healthcare administrators make informed decisions and improve overall service quality.
